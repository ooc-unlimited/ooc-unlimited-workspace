# /analyze-competitor — Research a Competing Agency/Recruiter

## Usage
`/analyze-competitor <company or recruiter name>`

## Instructions

Research the specified competing insurance agency or recruiter and produce a competitive analysis.

### 1. Research
Use web search to find:
- Company website, social media, job postings
- Their recruiting pitch and compensation structure
- Leadership team and key recruiters
- Reviews from agents (Glassdoor, Reddit, forums)
- Their market positioning and unique selling points
- Size, geography, carriers they work with

### 2. Generate Report
Save to `outputs/competitor-<slug>-YYYY-MM-DD.md`:

```markdown
# Competitor Analysis: <Name>
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Overview
<What they do, size, geography>

## Their Recruiting Pitch
<How they attract agents — comp, culture, tools, training>

## Compensation Structure
<What's known about their agent comp>

## Strengths
- ...

## Weaknesses
- ...

## How OOC Unlimited Compares
<Direct comparison on key dimensions>

## Opportunities
<How Gary can win agents from them or differentiate>

## Sources
- [links]
```

### 3. Summarize
Present key takeaways conversationally.
