## The 6 Touchpoint System

### Touchpoint 1: Day 1 (Text + Call)
**Purpose:** Introduce yourself, set expectations, build initial trust.

**Text:**
> "Hey [NAME]! It's [MENTOR]. Gary told me about you and I'm pumped to be your go-to person. I went through the exact same thing you're going through right now, so anything you need ‚Äî questions, venting, whatever ‚Äî I'm here. Let's hop on a quick 10-min call today or tomorrow. What works?"

**Call Agenda (10 min):**
1. Share YOUR story briefly (how you started, your first week feelings)
2. Ask about their WHY
3. Set expectations: "I'll check in on Days 3, 7, 14, 21, and 30. These aren't optional ‚Äî they're how we make sure you succeed."
4. Give them permission to struggle: "The first 2 weeks are the hardest. That's normal. Don't make any decisions about this business until Day 30."

### Touchpoint 2: Day 3 (Text)
**Purpose:** Check the emotional temperature. Did pushback happen?

> "Hey [NAME]! Quick check-in ‚Äî have you told anyone yet about what you're doing? What was their reaction? (Don't worry, whatever they said is normal. I'll tell you what happened to me üòÇ)"

**If they say nobody pushed back:** Great! It's coming though. Be ready.  
**If they got pushback:** Normalize it. Share your own story. Remind them it's a sign of courage, not a mistake.

### Touchpoint 3: Day 7 (Call ‚Äî 15 min)
**Purpose:** One week check-in. Identity reinforcement.

**Agenda:**
1. "On a scale of 1-10, how are you feeling about this decision?"
2. "What have you done this week?" (CE progress, group chat, etc.)
3. "Tell me about your WHY again." (Repetition locks identity)
4. Preview what's coming: "This next week is when the real stuff starts. We're going to get you in front of someone."

### Touchpoint 4: Day 14 (Call ‚Äî 15 min)
**Purpose:** Action check. First appointment should be happening around now.

**Agenda:**
1. "Have you had your first appointment yet?"
   - **Yes:** CELEBRATE. Ask how it went. Debrief the experience.
   - **No:** Don't shame. Ask what's blocking it. Offer to help schedule one.
2. "How's your prospect list? Have you reached out to any of your Top 5?"
3. If stuck: "What if we set up ONE appointment together this week? I'll walk you through the text to send."

**üö® If Day 14 and no CE complete, no appointments, no engagement ‚Üí flag as at-risk to Stacy/Gary.**

### Touchpoint 5: Day 21 (Text + Optional Call)
**Purpose:** Objection handling check. They've been in conversations by now.

> "3 weeks in! What objections are you hearing the most? From prospects? From yourself? Let's talk through them ‚Äî I've heard every single one and I've got answers for all of them."

**If they're producing:** Shift to growth mode. "Let's talk about getting to 5 appointments this week."  
**If they're not producing:** Direct conversation needed. "Real talk ‚Äî what's getting in the way? Let's figure this out together."

### Touchpoint 6: Day 30 (Call ‚Äî 20 min)
**Purpose:** 30-day review prep. Honest assessment.

**Agenda:**
1. "You've been in this for a month. Tell me honestly ‚Äî how do you feel?"
2. Review numbers: appointments, applications, commission earned
3. "What's been the hardest part?"
4. "What's been the best part?"
5. Prep for 30-Day Review with Gary: "Gary's going to ask you about your goals and your numbers. Be honest with him. He's not looking for perfection ‚Äî he's looking for commitment."

## After Day 30: Weekly Text Check-ins

From Day 30 through Day 90, send a weekly text every Monday:

**Template rotation:**
- Week 5: "New week, new opportunities. What's your #1 goal this week?"
- Week 6: "How many appointments do you have booked this week?"
- Week 7: "Wins from last week? Even small ones count. Drop them here."
- Week 8: "Grand Opening prep ‚Äî how's the invite list looking?"
- Week 9: "Almost at 60 days. How does it feel compared to Day 1?"
- Week 10-13: Continue rotating through goals, wins, accountability
