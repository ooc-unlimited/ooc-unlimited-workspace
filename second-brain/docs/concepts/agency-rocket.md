# Agency Rocket

## What It Is
Two-purpose tool used in Gary's CE Part 1 and CE Part 2 onboarding sessions.

## CE Part 1: Webinar
- Educational webinar shown to new agents during first training
- Covers the opportunity, compensation structure, and business model
- Played after Onboarding 1 slides, before Tevah PFR

## CE Part 2: Comparison Tool
- Retirement planning comparison tool
- Used to review the agent's financial numbers from their PFR (Tevah)
- Shows the gap between their current trajectory and what's possible
- Used to close internal consumption business (retirement plan)

## Pipeline Position
- **CE Part 1**: Onboarding 1 slides → **Agency Rocket webinar** → Tevah PFR → Ethos income protection
- **CE Part 2**: Review PFR numbers → **Agency Rocket comparison tool** → close business → schedule Onboarding 2
