## The Mindset Rules

1. **Speed beats perfection.** A decent appointment today beats a perfect one next month.
2. **Activity solves everything.** Feeling down? Make 5 calls. Feeling uncertain? Book an appointment. Action cures fear.
3. **Your first 10 appointments will be rough.** That's normal. Your next 10 will be better. Your next 10 after that will be natural.
4. **Celebrate every win.** First appointment. First "no." First "yes." First check. They all matter.
5. **The people who quit were one conversation away from their breakthrough.** Don't be that person.
