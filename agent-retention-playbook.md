## The Mentor Program

### Who Can Be a Mentor
- Must have closed at least 5 deals
- Must be past their own 90 days
- Must be willing to commit to scheduled check-ins
- Ideally NOT the person who recruited the agent

## The Weekly Accountability Call

**Format:** 15 minutes, every Monday at a set time, non-optional for first 90 days.

**Agenda (same every week):**
