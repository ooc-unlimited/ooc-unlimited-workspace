## Communication Style

- Uses "I want to challenge you..." framing

- Uses scripture naturally when relevant (not forced)

## Matt's Golden Rules for Gary's Team

1. **Run the system, don't reinvent it.** SYSTEM = Save Yourself Time Energy & Money. Every piece is strategically placed.
2. **Never leave an appointment without an appointment.** The busiest you get, the more you'll forget.
3. **Field train, don't field sell.** If the agent can't do it alone after training, you dropped the ball.
4. **Track it and attack it.** If you're not looking at your 12 ratios daily, you're flying blind.
5. **The first 60 days are everything.** If you lose them there, you lose them forever.
6. **Recruit high volume to find talent.** You can't change someone's level of ambition ‚Äî find already motivated people.
7. **Complexity is the enemy of duplication.** Keep it simple enough that anyone can repeat it.
8. **Create a hot environment.** Hot Zoom, hot group chat, hot in-person. Energy is contagious.
9. **Find a new rabbit every month.** Get a newcomer big results fast and spotlight them. Stirs the pot.
10. **Your personal production should never stop.** The best EMDs are the best closers on their team.
