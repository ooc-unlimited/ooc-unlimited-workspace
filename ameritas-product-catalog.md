## 12. Ameritas ClearEdge Living Benefits Term
- **File**: `GetFile (9).pdf`
- **Document ID**: LI 2591
- **Date**: January 2025
- **Type**: Producer Product Guide
- **Category**: Level Term Life with Living Benefits
- **Audience**: Financial Professionals
- **Term Periods**: 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 years
- **Issue Ages**: Varies by term & band (18-80 max for 10yr Band 2+; 18-50 for 30yr Band 1)
- **Face Amount**: Band 1 $50K–$99K (Std NT/T only); Bands 2-5 $100K–$1M+ (all 6 UW classes)
- **Table Ratings**: A-J; Flat extras $1-$25 per thousand
- **Living Benefits (Built-in ABR)**: Accelerate up to 90% of death benefit (max $1.5M) for:
  - **Terminal Illness**: Life expectancy 12 months or less
  - **Chronic Illness**: Unable to perform 2 of 6 ADLs for 90+ days, or severe cognitive impairment
  - **Critical Illness**: 15 qualifying conditions (cancer, heart attack, stroke, ALS, organ transplant, etc.)
- **Convertibility**: Years 3-5 (all terms) to full permanent; years 6-10/15/20 by term length to age 65
- **Riders**: ABR (terminal/chronic/critical), Accidental Death, Children's Insurance ($25K), Waiver of Premium
- **Discounts**: Association (4%), Employee (4%), Same Payor (50% policy fee)
- **Key Differentiator**: Most comprehensive built-in living benefits of any Ameritas term product
- **Carrier**: Ameritas Life Insurance Corp.
